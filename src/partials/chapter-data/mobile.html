But it doesn’t stop there. You can get a more complete view of your customer by unlocking other data sources trapped in silos like 3rd party data lakes and warehouses, that contain valuable data related to your customer.

Data Cloud unifies all of a company's data on Salesforce, giving every team a 360-degree view of the customer to power automation, personalized engagement, and trusted AI.

Salesforce Data Cloud makes it easy for us to map the incoming data from other systems into this shared data model.

Using Data Cloud, you can easily harmonize all your data with the Salesforce metadata framework, through a simple point and click mapping interface. This turns data from any source into native Salesforces objects and fields that can be easily used to surface relevant insights or power actions within applications like Sales, Service, and Marketing Cloud.
