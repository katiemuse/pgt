Create a Custom Action

Now that you've selected which components to include in your app, let's customize the action bar to make it even easier for your users to work on mobile. Salesforce1 already comes with built-in actions, but you can also create custom actions tailored to your own business process and use cases.

Did You Know?

Actions enable users to do more in Salesforce and in Salesforce1. Think of them as shortcuts. They offer a fast way for mobile users to launch a specific workflow in Salesforce1, like creating records, logging calls, or sharing files.
