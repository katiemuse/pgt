Build a Mobile App for Product Managers

Using Lightning App Builder, our drag and drop development environment, we will build a mobile app for our Product Managers that surfaces all that customer data, processes, and actions you created earlier.

Salesforce Platform lets you build apps that are mobile-first and automatically connected to your CRM data.

App Page

Use an app page to create a home page for a third-party app that you can add directly into the Salesforce1 and Lightning Experience navigation menus. Your users then have an app home page where they can quickly access the most important objects and items.

Home Page

Create Home pages with features relevant to specific types of users, and assign the customized pages to different user profiles. Custom Home pages are supported in Lightning Experience only.

Record Page

With a record page, you can create a customized version of an object’s record page, tailoring it to your users’ needs. Custom record pages are supported in Lightning Experience only.

Did You Know?

We call them 'pages' in Lightning, but these pages are just as powerful as any app, and your users can access them through Salesforce1 or Salesforce Lightning.

Did You Know?

Base components come standard with Force.com, but you can add Third Party components from the AppExchange or even add custom components built by your development team.
