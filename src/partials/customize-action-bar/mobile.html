View a Prospect Profile

See how prospects are interacting with your marketing campaigns by viewing their activities, lead score, and lead grade in their prospect profiles.

Let's say you've set up some forms, landing pages, and email templates.

What happens when users interact with your marketing programs by submitting their information via a Pardot form, viewing a landing page, or clicking on a link in one of your emails?

All of this information is tracked in Pardot and displayed in each prospect’s profile, so you can stay up to date on your hottest prospects.

Lead Score and Grade

Here, you can see both a lead score and grade. A prospect’s score indicates how interested they are in your product or service. The score increases as prospects accumulate activities.

A prospect’s grade, on the other hand, indicates whether or not they fit your ideal prospect profile.

This is determined by comparing the prospect’s demographic information to criteria you set, including job titles, industries, location, and more.

Together, score and grade help you identify your most qualified leads.

Prospect's Activities

Pardot will compile a detailed list of your prospect’s activities and display them on the prospect profile.

You can see how they’ve been interacting with your email content, what pages they’ve viewed on your website, and more.
