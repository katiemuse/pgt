Predict Customers’ Net Promoter Scores

We will use Einstein Builder to create a field that predicts how a specific type of customer case impacts the customer’s Net Promoter Score. Einstein makes the prediction based on a few things you choose, like number of device crashes, installation issues, and escalations. *
