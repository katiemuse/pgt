A/B Testing

With A/B testing, you can alter the content of an email, deploy two versions, and then test to see which performs most successfully.

Continue

Did You Know?

The two versions of your email will be distinguished from each other using blue and orange coloring, as you can see here.

Did You Know?

With Pardot A/B testing, you can test your subject lines, images, body copy, layout, email sender, and more.

Did You Know?

You can determine how long you want the A/B testing period to last.

After a certain percentage of your emails have deployed (decided by you), Pardot will select the winning email to send to the remaining email recipients on your list.
