Emails are the foundation of your drip campaigns. Using the dropdown menu in the drip builder, you can choose from any Pardot email template that you have designated as a “drip email.“

Pause 9 Days

A “pause” indicates how long Pardot will wait before carrying out the next step in your drip program.

For sales nurturing campaigns, an email every week is too frequent, but an email every two weeks may not be enough. Nine days is a good cadence to help maintain a balance between “too much communication” and “not enough.”

Assign to User

If the prospect engaged with your email, it might be a good time for a sales rep to follow up. Assign them to a sales user to let your sales reps know that a follow-up is needed.

Did You Know?

You can use personalized variable tags so that your drip emails will address a prospect by their first name, mention their company, and have the assigned sales rep's specific contact information included in the salutation.

Did You Know?

You can use plain text emails in sales drip programs in order to create a more personal feel, as if emails were personally composed by a sales rep.
