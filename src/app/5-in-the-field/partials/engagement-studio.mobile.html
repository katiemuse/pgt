Engagement Campaigns

Build nurturing campaigns to keep your prospects engaged over time.

Let’s say a prospect has filled out a few forms, downloaded some of your marketing content, but isn’t quite ready to talk to a sales rep yet. This is the perfect use case for lead nurturing, which allows you to encourage engagement and stay top of mind with your customers. Engagement campaigns are personalized and dynamic, allowing you to build relationship at scale.

Did You Know?

Engagement Studio is a streamlined way to build complex campaigns and deliver them to highly segmented audiences. The more you learn about your audience, the better your campaign gets!

Build Smarter Campaigns

Pardot’s visual campaign canvas makes building your perfect engagement campaign a snap. At a glance you can understand exactly which path your customers will take as they interact with your brand.
