Pardot Campaigns

You can choose to associate your forms with Pardot campaigns in order to better track their success — and keep your marketing assets organized.

Pardot’s drag-and-drop form builder makes it easy to determine which form fields you would like to use on your form.

Did You Know?

Pardot gives users the ability to use “progressive profiling” on forms. Progressive profiling allows you to collect more data over time by dynamically displaying form fields based on whether or not a user has already filled them out. For example, if a user has already submitted their job title, the next form they encounter can ask for their industry instead.

Customize Form Fields

With Pardot, form fields are completely customizable, so you can choose the fields that are most applicable to your audience.

Once you've created a form, it’s time for the fun part: creating a landing page using Pardot's WYSIWYG landing page builder. You can use landing pages in tandem with forms to “gate” marketing assets like white papers and product demos, making them essential components of your lead generation strategy.

Landing Pages

Pardot makes it easy to design beautiful landing pages without having to know HTML

Did You Know?

Pardot's landing page builder allows you to import the HTML from existing pages on your website, making it easy to create landing pages that match the look and feel of your brand.

Pardot also comes pre-loaded with several landing page templates for you to choose from — or you can create your own!
