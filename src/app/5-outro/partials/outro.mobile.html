Your App in Action

We've walked through the app-creation process, now let's see what it can do. The Customer 360 app enables users to:

Take a detailed look at sales orders over time.

Receive immediate alerts when customers provide feedback.

Dive into customer records.

Add customer ideas to their team's backlog of workstreams.

Check out their backlog at a glance, and at individual issues in detail, while moving them down the queue.

Give customers the attention they expect and deserve.

And with that, you can take customer feedback from ideas to action inside a single app. It's that simple!
