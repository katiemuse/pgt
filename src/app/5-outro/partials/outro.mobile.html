Pardot Reporting

Tie closed deals back to their original campaigns to gauge the success of your marketing and sales efforts.

With some help from Pardot and Salesforce® Engage, your sales reps will be well on their way to bringing in a flood of new customers. But as a marketer, you still want to look back at your campaigns to see what worked and what didn’t. Pardot reporting can help.

Let’s take a look at two Pardot reports that will help you gauge your campaign success. We'll start with the Campaign Performance Overview report, then dive deeper with advanced email reporting.

Campaign Performance Overview

The Campaign Performance Overview report gives a high-level overview of your campaign’s overall health in comparison to other campaigns. Each campaign is represented by a colored bubble in the chart below.

Did You Know?

Pardot comes with more than twenty out-of-the-box reports, including paid search reporting, content and social reports, landing page reports, sales funnel reporting, and more.

Advanced Email Report

Pardot’s advanced email reporting provides insight into email engagement, email clients and devices used by recipients, deliverability rates, click-through rates, and more.

Did You Know?

Pardot email reports can tell you whether your emails have been read, skimmed, or ignored by your email recipients. You can also see a breakdown of the email clients, browsers, and devices used to open your emails.

Click-Through Rate Report

Get a visual look at your click-through rates with Pardot’s click-through rate report. This report features a visual heat map with links colored on a scale from red to blue to quickly determine the most highly trafficked links.

Email Client Report

View a breakdown of the email clients and devices used to open your emails (hint: your designers and developers will love this report).

Did You Know?

Pardot’s device reporting captures and tracks email client usage across 120+ email clients.
