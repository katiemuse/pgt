Did You Know?

Landing pages are a great lead generation tool. Use them in tandem with forms to "gate" marketing assets like white papers and product demos. When a visitor submits their information to access your content, their data is automatically reflected in their prospect profile in Pardot. Did You Know?

Landing pages allow you to better track the success of each of your marketing channels. Trace each form submission to a specific landing page to see which of your channels is converting the best.

Did You Know?

The Pardot landing page builder allows you to import the HTML from existing pages on your website, making it easy to create landing pages that match the look and feel of your brand.
