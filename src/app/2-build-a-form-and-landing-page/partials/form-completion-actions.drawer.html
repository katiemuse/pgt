Completion Actions

Completion actions are actions that will automatically trigger when someone submits one of your forms.

For example, you can set up a completion action to send an email with a downloadable asset if someone submits a form in exchange for a white paper (note: this type of email is called an autoresponder).

Let's take a look at all of the possible completion actions you can use with Pardot forms.
