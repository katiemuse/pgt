Salesforce® Engage

Connect sales to customers like never before using Salesforce® Engage.

If you’re a sales rep, you don’t want to waste time jumping between sales applications, especially when your quota is on the line.

With Salesforce® Engage, you can keep tabs on your active prospects in real time, send 1:1 emails to prospects using pre-approved templates or plain text, report on the success of these emails, and more — right from within your CRM.

Engage Alerts

Engage Alerts provides a customizable feed of real-time prospect activities, so that sales reps are always up-to-date on the latest actionable information. In addition, real-time alerts will appear as pop-up notifications on your desktop when a visitor or prospect takes an action.

Did You Know?

View only the most relevant activity on your Engage Alerts feed by customizing your own filters based on geography, activity type, and more.

You can also view a list of your prospect activities on-the-go from within the Salesforce® Engage mobile application on Salesforce1.

With Salesforce® Engage, it's easy to send 1:1 emails, or "Engage Campaigns," to your prospects. You can either choose a template that's been pre-created by your marketing team, or create a plain text email from scratch.

You can customize your send lists by adding or removing email recipients within Salesforce® Engage.

Did You Know?

Marketing can place a limit on the number of emails that sales reps can send on a daily basis.

Using the Salesforce® Engage mobile application within Salesforce1, you can add your prospects to nurture campaigns with just a few simple taps.

Engage Reports

Salesforce® Engage includes Engage Reporting to give you insight into your email open rates, click-through rates, and more. You can also drill down to see who is opening your emails and which links they’re clicking on.

Did You Know?

Engage campaign reporting allows you to see which templates are performing best. Restrict your reports to templated or non-templated emails to get even more granular with your reporting.

In addition to Engage reporting, Pardot offers a number of out-of-the-box marketing reports.
