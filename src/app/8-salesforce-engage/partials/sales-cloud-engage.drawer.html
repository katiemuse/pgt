Salesforce® Engage

If you're a sales rep, you don't want to waste time jumping between sales applications, especially when your quota is on the line. Pardot's Salesforce® Engage allows you to take care of all of your selling tasks right from within your CRM.

With Salesforce® Engage, you can keep tabs on your active prospects in real time, send 1:1 emails to prospects using pre-approved templates or plain text, report on the success of these emails, and more.

Did You Know?

You can use icons above each Engage Alert slider to pause them, change their settings, delete them, or toggle notifications on or off. The colors on each slide represent a different activity so you can quickly glance at the cards and get an overall sense of your prospect's actions.

Did You Know?

Engage Alerts provides real-time alerts of prospect activities, which will appear as pop-up notifications at the top right of your desktop when a visitor or prospect takes an action.

Did You Know?

You can also view a list of your prospect activities on-the-go from within Salesforce® Engage mobile application on Salesforce1.
