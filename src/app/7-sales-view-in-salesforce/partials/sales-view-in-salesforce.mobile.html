Sales View in Salesforce

Your sales reps will be able to view prospect scores, grades, and activities in Salesforce.

After a lead has been assigned to sales, the fate of that lead passes into the hands of your sales reps. But don’t worry, all of the data collected by Pardot is available to your sales reps, and they don’t even have to leave Salesforce to access it.

Salesforce Contact Record

You can see your prospect’s score and grade in the Salesforce contact record. This helps sales reps prioritize their hottest leads at a glance.

Did You Know?

Syncing between Pardot and Salesforce ensures that all records are up-to-date across both tools.

Prospect Activities Log

See a log of prospect activities, including website browsing activities, interactions with marketing campaigns, call history, and more.

Add Prospects to Lists

Add individual prospects to lists from within Salesforce. This allows sales reps to enroll prospects in drip campaigns if further nurturing is needed, or remove them from lists if necessary.
