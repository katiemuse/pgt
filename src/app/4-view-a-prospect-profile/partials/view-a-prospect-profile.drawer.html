Prospect's Score

A prospect's score indicates how interested they are in your product or service. The score increases as prospects accumulate activities.

Pardot's scoring model is fully customizable to your business needs.

Prospect's Grade


A prospect's grade indicates whether or not they fit your ideal prospect profile.

This is determined by comparing the prospect's demographic information to criteria you set, including job titles, industries, location, and more.

Activities List

Pardot will compile a detailed list of your prospect's activities and display them in this chart.

You can see how they've been interacting with your email content, what pages they've viewed on your website, and more.

Did You Know?

If you don't know the company that your prospect works for, Pardot's anonymous visitor tracking can perform various lookups using the prospect's IP address.
