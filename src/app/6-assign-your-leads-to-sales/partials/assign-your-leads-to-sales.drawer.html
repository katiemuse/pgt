Assign Your Leads to Sales

Pardot uses automation rules to automate lead assignment, simplifying the lead pass process for both sales and marketers.

Let’s walk through a sample automation rule that will trigger lead assignment to sales reps once they reach a threshold score and grade.

Continue

Score

You can input the score you would like to use as a threshold here. Remember, a score tells you how active a prospect has been, and therefore how interested they are in your product or service.

Grade

Input the grade you would like to set as a threshold here. Grades vary from A+ to F-, so a grade of B or A is generally the most appropriate. As a reminder, a grade indicates how well the lead fits your ideal prospect profile.

Did You Know?

Automation rules can be used for more than assigning leads. You can also use them to segment prospects onto lists, remove prospects from lists, create tasks in Salesforce, adjust scores, and more.
