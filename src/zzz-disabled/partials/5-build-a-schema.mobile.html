Create a Campaign

Let's start by looking at Pardot's campaign builder. Creating a campaign is simple — all you have to do to launch your very own marketing campaign is input a name and cost.

Did You Know?

A “campaign” is used to determine the first touch that a prospect has with your marketing programs. That could be a marketing email, a white paper, a Google advertisement, or any other marketing channel!

Organizing with Tags

You can add ‘tags’ to your marketing campaigns to help keep them organized. Tags are great for grouping related campaigns together — and they make searching a breeze.

ROI

Inputting your initial cost will help determine your campaign ROI at the end of this experience.

After creating a campaign in Pardot, you can move on to create forms and landing pages. Click "continue" to see what this process looks like.
